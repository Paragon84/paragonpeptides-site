Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

I had an issue with my checkout while using a coupon, and now my coupon won’t work, what should I do?

In this case please wait an hour for our system to realise the coupon wasn’t used, and the coupon will automatically refresh and be ready for use again! If you have waited an hour and the coupon still doesn’t work, please reach out to support and we’ll send you a new code.

Where are your COAs?

For research compounds sold strictly for laboratory use, we do not release Certificates of Analysis (COAs) publicly or upon request.

Our products are HPLC-verified in-house at ≥99% purity before every batch ships. However, we do not provide third-party COAs for the following reasons:

  • Regulatory compliance – Releasing COAs could imply the material is intended for human or clinical use, which violates our research-only policy and could create legal risk for both us and our customers.
  • Customer privacy & security – Third-party lab reports contain sensitive batch data. We protect that information to prevent counterfeiters from copying our exact testing standards.
  • Speed & focus – Every order is tested internally before it leaves our facility. We prioritize fast cold-chain shipping over paperwork that most researchers never need.

If you require independent verification for your specific project, you can always send a small sample from your vial to any accredited lab (MZ Biolabs, Janoshik, or Prime are commonly used by the community). We stand behind our in-house testing and offer a full refund if any third-party test shows purity below 98%.

All products on Paragon Peptides are sold exclusively for scientific research purposes. Thank you for understanding.
